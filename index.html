Our Story

Where Every Dish
is a Magical Act

Nestled in the historic heart of Bandel, Hooghly, Barfi - The Food Magician is more than a restaurant — it's an experience. We blend the soul of Bengali culinary tradition with bold global flavours, conjuring dishes that surprise, delight, and linger long after the last bite.

Every ingredient is sourced fresh daily, every recipe crafted with intention, and every plate presented as a small act of wonder. Welcome to our table.
